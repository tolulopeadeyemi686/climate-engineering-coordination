# Climate Engineering Coordination

## Overview

The Climate Engineering Coordination platform is a global geoengineering platform built on the Stacks blockchain that coordinates climate intervention projects with transparent impact tracking and international consensus mechanisms. This revolutionary system enables coordinated climate action through decentralized governance and verifiable environmental impact measurements.

## Project Description

Inspired by Project Vesta's beach sand enhancement and Climeworks' direct air capture technologies, combined with the coordination needs demonstrated by international climate agreements, this platform provides the infrastructure for transparent, verifiable, and coordinated global climate interventions.

### Key Features

- **Global Climate Intervention Coordination**: Coordinate massive geoengineering projects across nations
- **Transparent Impact Tracking**: Real-time monitoring and verification of environmental impact
- **International Consensus Mechanisms**: Democratic voting systems for climate intervention approval
- **Carbon Removal Verification**: Blockchain-verified carbon capture and removal claims
- **Climate Intervention Credits**: Tokenized system for trading climate intervention outcomes

## Smart Contracts

### Geo Coordinator Contract
The core smart contract that handles:
- Coordination of global climate engineering projects and initiatives
- Real-time tracking of environmental impact from climate interventions
- Management of international consensus voting for intervention approval
- Verification of carbon removal claims through decentralized oracles
- Distribution of climate intervention credits based on verified impact

## Technology Stack

- **Blockchain**: Stacks (Bitcoin Layer 2)
- **Smart Contract Language**: Clarity
- **Development Framework**: Clarinet
- **Environmental Data**: IoT sensor integration
- **Verification**: Decentralized oracle networks

## Real-World Applications

This platform addresses critical climate action needs:
- **Coordinated Geoengineering**: Large-scale climate interventions require international cooperation
- **Impact Verification**: Transparent measurement and verification of climate action effectiveness
- **Carbon Credit Markets**: Reliable tracking and trading of carbon removal outcomes
- **International Cooperation**: Democratic mechanisms for global climate action approval
- **Emergency Climate Response**: Rapid coordination for climate emergency interventions

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Clarinet CLI installed
- Node.js and npm
- Git

### Installation
1. Clone the repository
2. Install dependencies: `npm install`
3. Run tests: `clarinet test`
4. Check contracts: `clarinet check`

## Contract Architecture

The system is built around a central geo-coordinator contract that manages:
- Climate intervention project registration and coordination
- Environmental impact measurement and verification
- International consensus voting mechanisms
- Carbon removal claim verification and tracking
- Climate intervention credit distribution and trading

## Economic Model

- **Project Funding**: International funding pools for climate intervention projects
- **Impact Verification**: Economic incentives for accurate environmental impact measurement
- **Carbon Credits**: Tokenized carbon removal outcomes for trading and offsetting
- **Consensus Rewards**: Incentives for participation in democratic climate governance
- **Emergency Funding**: Rapid response funding for climate emergencies

## Security Features

- **Multi-nation Consensus**: Prevent unilateral climate interventions through democratic approval
- **Impact Verification**: Multiple independent verification of environmental claims
- **Transparent Governance**: All decisions and impact data publicly verifiable
- **Emergency Protocols**: Secure emergency response mechanisms for climate crises
- **Anti-manipulation**: Prevent gaming of carbon credit and impact systems
